Call to Glory (detto o' muort che parla)

w/ Alessandra Pedrini and Bendetta Riccio

Call to Glory (detto o' muort che parla) is a typographic and iconographic research on obituaries and death notices around the world, in a period of time where the only way to travel around was by using Google Street View and where the idea of mortality pervaded and shaped our daily life. The work documents the ways in which the "glorious home call" is advertised in different cultures, focusing on the typography of death, which usually leads to the death of typography itself.

(2020)
